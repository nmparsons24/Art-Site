Art is more than what you see. It is the emotions, the story, and the materials that we percieve with more than just our eyes. Using offcuts from the small scale canvas bag making of Ratbags on the Isles of Scilly, Nancy's pieces give a sense of place only achieved through her love of observing a moment and stitching it into permenance.
